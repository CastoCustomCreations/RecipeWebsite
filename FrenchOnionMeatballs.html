French Onion Meatballs

Prep Time Cook Time Total Time
20 mins 1 hr 20 min 1 hrs 40 mins

Servings: 6 (24 meatballs)

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 lb ground beef
  • 1 1/2 Tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1 can(s) French onion soup, 10.5 oz.
  • 1 pkg au jus gravy mix, 1 oz.
  • 2 c water
  • 1 c beef broth
  • shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 2 yellow onions, cooked until caramelized
  • Sub Rolls

Instructions

Combine ground beef, Worcestershire sauce and salt and pepper. Form into about 24 meatballs. Bake meatballs in a 350-degree oven for about 20 minutes or until done. In Crock Pot combine au jus mix, water, and beef broth. Whisk together, Add French onion soup and meatballs into the Crock Pot. Heat on high an hour or until heated through.
While everything is in the Crock Pot, caramelize the onions in a skillet. Toast rolls in a skillet with butter. Serve on sub rolls with cheese and caramelized onions. Use any condiments you would like on the sub, we like mayonnaise or horseradish.
