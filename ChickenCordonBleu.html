Chicken Cordon Bleu Meatballs

Prep Time Cook Time Total Time
20 mins 20 min 40 mins

Servings: 8

Ingredients

    Meatballs

  • 2 pounds ground chicken
  • 6 ounces grated swiss cheese
  • 6 ounces ham diced
  • 1 egg
  • 2/3 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon of sea salt


    Mustard Sour Cream Sauce

  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 3 tablespoons mustard
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

Instructions

For the meatballs, preheat the oven to 400F and prepare a baking sheet with a baking rack inside for cooking. Add the ham, egg, breadcrumbs, and spices to a food processor. Blend together to form a paste, the ham should be ground similar to the chicken. Add the ham mixture to a large bowl with the chicken and cheese. Mix with your hands to distribute the ham into the chicken, then form into 35-40 meatballs, about 1 1/2 inches in diameter. Place the meatballs on the baking rack inside the baking sheet. Bake the meatballs for 22-25 minutes at 400F. While the meatballs are baking, combine the ingredients for the mustard sauce in a bowl. Taste, and add more salt or mustard to your preference. Remove the meatballs from the oven and let cool for a minute or two, then serve with the sauce for dipping. Enjoy!
